Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description: Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, comforting, and quick to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  4. Remove the skillet from heat and add the cooked spaghetti to the pancetta.
  5. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing to combine and create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  6. Season with freshly ground black pepper and salt to taste.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Pecorino Romano cheese if desired.
