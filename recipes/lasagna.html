Lasagna Recipe

Description: Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn’t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses as follows:
    • 12 noodles
    • 1 cup meat sauce
    • 1 cup ricotta cheese
    • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
    • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce and grated parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
